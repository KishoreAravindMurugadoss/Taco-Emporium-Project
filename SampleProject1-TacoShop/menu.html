Our Menu

  1. Tacos Arabes Puebla

    Discription:

    Tacos Árabes Puebla is a mouthwatering culinary delight that merges Middle Eastern and Mexican flavors into a harmonious fusion. Originating in the vibrant city of Puebla, Mexico, this iconic dish features succulent slices of marinated pork, traditionally cooked on a vertical spit, reminiscent of the beloved shawarma. The tender and juicy meat is then tucked inside a soft, warm tortilla and garnished with a vibrant medley of fresh ingredients such as diced onions, aromatic cilantro, and zesty salsa. The result is a tantalizing explosion of flavors, where the smoky, spiced pork perfectly complements the vibrant, herbaceous toppings. Tacos Árabes Puebla is an exquisite testament to the rich cultural exchange and culinary creativity that defines Mexican cuisine.

  2. Carne Asada Tacos

    Discription:

    Carne Asada Tacos are a beloved staple of Mexican cuisine that tantalize the senses with their sizzling and savory goodness. Originating from the northern regions of Mexico, this dish features thinly sliced, marinated steak grilled to perfection, imbued with flavors of garlic, lime, and various spices. The tender and juicy meat is then nestled in warm corn tortillas, providing the perfect vessel to hold the delicious filling. Accompanied by a medley of vibrant toppings such as diced onions, cilantro, and tangy salsa, each bite of Carne Asada Tacos bursts with a harmonious combination of smoky, charred flavors and fresh, zesty notes. Whether enjoyed with a squeeze of lime or a dollop of creamy guacamole, these tacos are a true crowd-pleaser, showcasing the rich culinary heritage and inviting warmth of Mexican street food.

  3. Tacos Al Pastor

    Discription:

    Tacos al pastor, a beloved culinary gem hailing from Mexico, are a tantalizing feast for the senses. These succulent, marinated pork tacos transport one's taste buds to a realm of smoky, savory bliss. Traditionally prepared by slow-cooking marinated pork on a vertical spit, the meat is thinly sliced and nestled inside a soft corn tortilla, accompanied by vibrant toppings like fresh cilantro, diced onions, and zesty pineapple chunks. The harmonious fusion of flavors, with hints of tanginess, spiciness, and a touch of sweetness, creates an irresistible symphony that captures the heart of every taco lover. With every bite, tacos al pastor deliver an unforgettable culinary experience that celebrates the rich heritage and passion for food found in Mexican cuisine.

  4. Barbacoa Tacos

    Discription:

    Barbacoa tacos, a culinary treasure deeply rooted in Mexican tradition, exude a tantalizing aroma and exquisite flavor that lingers in the memory long after the last bite. Derived from the ancient method of slow-cooking meat underground, barbacoa showcases tender, succulent beef or lamb that has been meticulously seasoned and braised to perfection. The result is a melt-in-your-mouth texture, infused with a symphony of spices and herbs, like cumin, garlic, and chilies, that create a robust and savory profile. Encased in warm, freshly made corn tortillas and adorned with vibrant toppings such as diced onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime, barbacoa tacos offer a harmonious balance of rich, smoky flavors that truly transport one's palate to the heart of Mexican culinary heritage. Whether enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, each bite of a barbacoa taco is a celebration of the artistry and passion that goes into creating this beloved dish.

  5. Tacos de Buche

    Discription:


    Tacos de buche, a beloved specialty in Mexican cuisine, are a mouthwatering delight that captivate the senses with their unique flavors and textures. Prepared using tender and succulent pig stomach, the buche is marinated in a blend of aromatic spices and then grilled to perfection, resulting in a tantalizing combination of smoky, savory, and slightly chewy goodness. Served on warm, soft tortillas and garnished with vibrant salsa, onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime, tacos de buche offer a delightful balance of richness, tanginess, and freshness that make them a true culinary treasure, capturing the essence of authentic Mexican street food.

  6. Birria Tacos

    Discription:

    Birria tacos are a mouthwatering culinary delight that originated in Mexico and have gained popularity worldwide. These tantalizing tacos feature tender and flavorful shredded meat, traditionally made from beef or goat, slow-cooked with a blend of aromatic spices, including chili peppers, garlic, cumin, and Mexican oregano. The meat is typically bathed in a rich and savory consommé, enhancing its succulence and imparting a delightful depth of flavors. Served on warm corn tortillas, birria tacos are often accompanied by diced onions, fresh cilantro, and a squeeze of lime, adding brightness and balance to the indulgent meaty goodness. With their irresistible combination of tender meat, aromatic spices, and vibrant toppings, birria tacos offer a truly unforgettable culinary experience for taco lovers around the world.

  7. Tacos de Cabeza

    Discription:

    Tacos de cabeza, or "head tacos," are a mouthwatering and beloved Mexican street food delicacy. This flavorful culinary creation features tender and succulent meat sourced from the head of various animals, such as beef or pork. The meat, usually cheek or tongue, is slow-cooked until it becomes incredibly tender, then it is seasoned with a delectable blend of spices and served on warm, corn tortillas. Topped with fresh cilantro, diced onions, tangy salsa, and a squeeze of lime, tacos de cabeza offer a combination of rich flavors and textures that make them a true delight for taco enthusiasts everywhere.
